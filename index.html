Cupcake Collector

  • You will be given a random number at the start of the game.
  • There are four cupcakes below. By clicking on a cupcake you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.
  • You win the game by matching your total score to the random number, you lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.
  • The value of each cupcake is hidden from you until you click on it.
  • Each time the game starts, the game will change the values of each cupcake.
